USC Student

Autobiography

 I’m Chin. Eddie is my English name. Live in Banciao. Born in May,31. I’m 18 years old,studying in the public Shilin High School of Commerce. This school is very huge. I come from a very happy middle class family. My father works at the school and my mother is a housekeeper.

 When I enter into high school for study, I have chance to go to USA in summer study tour for one month. This is very impressive experinence in my life. First I can touch the real American lifestyle, second my homepa was very close to me. I could talk to him and knowing their life value. And I got a lot of friends in this tour. So,this is very precious time to learn English.

Eddie
